Card recommendations — review annually
Based on your spend profile and current card lineup. Revisit each December.
Keep — Amex Gold Keep
4x dining on $10K/yr = $400 value. $120 dining credit + $120 Uber Cash offsets most of $250 AF. Net cost ~$10/yr for 4x dining. Easy keep.
Keep — Chase Sapphire Reserve Keep
$300 travel credit covers most of $550 AF. 10x hotel via Chase portal on $20K travel = $1,000+ value. Unlocks Chase point transfers to Hyatt/United. Essential anchor card.
Keep — Chase Freedom Unlimited Keep
No AF. 1.5x on everything — perfect default for home improvement ($30K/yr) and uncategorized spend. $750/yr in points at current spend. Free to hold.
Keep — Chase Freedom Flex Keep
No AF. 5x rotating categories — groceries + gym + Amazon in Q2 alone worth $1,150 in points. Only effort is quarterly activation. Free money.
Cards worth considering to add
Consider — Chase Ink Business Preferred Consider
If you launch consulting practice — 3x on business spend, large sign-up bonus (80-100K points), $95 AF. Business expenses on a dedicated card also simplifies tax tracking. Worth it once you have 5+ clients.
Consider — Amex Blue Cash Preferred Consider
6% on US grocery stores (up to $6K/yr) vs 4x Amex Gold. If grocery spend is primarily at US supermarkets, could beat Gold for that category. $95 AF. Evaluate when Freedom Flex grocery 5x doesn't apply (Q3/Q4).
Skip — Additional hotel cards Skip
Hyatt, Marriott, Hilton cards add AF complexity. Chase Reserve already gives you transfer access to all major hotel programs. No need to add another card just for hotel points.
