Keep — Amex Gold Keep
4x dining on $10K/yr = $400 value. $120 dining + $120 Uber Cash credits offset most of $250 AF. Net cost ~$10/yr for 4x dining. Easy keep.
Keep — Chase Sapphire Reserve Keep
$300 travel credit offsets most of $550 AF. 5x Chase Travel on $20K = $1,000+ value. Unlocks Chase point transfers to Hyatt/United. Essential anchor card.
Keep — Chase Freedom Unlimited Keep
No AF. 1.5x on everything — perfect default for home improvement ($30K/yr) and uncategorized spend. ~$750/yr in points. Free to hold.
Keep — Chase Freedom Keep
No AF. 5x rotating — Q2 Amazon + Chase Travel alone worth significant points. Only effort is quarterly activation. Free money.
Worth considering
Consider — Chase Ink Business Preferred Consider
If consulting practice launches — 3x on business spend, large sign-up bonus (80-100K points), $95 AF. Simplifies tax tracking too. Worth it once you have 5+ clients.
Skip — Hotel co-brand cards Skip
Chase Reserve already gives transfer access to Hyatt, Marriott, IHG. Adding hotel-specific AF cards adds complexity without meaningful incremental value at your travel spend.
